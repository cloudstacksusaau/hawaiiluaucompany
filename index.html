Hawaii Luau Company, LLC

Hawaii Luau Company is comprised of Hawaiian cultural experts Kaniala and Melody Masoe, along with veteran show producers and director, Jeff Gitlin and Larry Pellegrini. We are a fully encompassed luau, accounting, sales, marketing and production company, with over 20+ years of creating the highest quality Hawaiian Entertainment. Hawaii Luau Company is the production company which created “Huaka’i – Journey Through Polynesia,” which had a 6-year weekly performance run at the Ka’anapali Beach Club Resort in Maui, Hawaii. As veteran producers of luaus in Hawaii, we are happy to present our vision for a highly entertaining, yet true depiction of a Hawaiian Luau for Seascape Restaurant at Maalaea Harbor at the Maui Ocean Center. We recognize the importance of highlighting the ocean conservation objective… to showcase a luau reflective of its Hawaiian spirit regarding the preservation of the Hawaiian Oceans. We are happy to tell the story of the Hawaiian Islands and the Journey Through Polynesia.
